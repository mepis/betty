# open-source-tts

Open-source text-to-speech models have reached near-parity with commercial APIs in 2026, offering significant advantages in cost, customization, and deployment flexibility. Leading models include Kokoro 82M v1.0 (Apache-2.0, $0.65/1M chars), Qwen3-TTS (10 languages, emotion control), Chatterbox Turbo (MIT, paralinguistic expressions), and Fish Speech S2 Pro (80+ languages).
