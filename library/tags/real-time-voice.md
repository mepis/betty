# real-time-voice

Real-time voice AI refers to voice systems that process and respond within human-perceptible latency thresholds (typically under 500ms). Achieving real-time voice interaction requires optimized STT, low-latency LLM inference, streaming TTS, and sophisticated orchestration including sentence aggregation and pipelined execution.
